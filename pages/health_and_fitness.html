Exercise routine

We take the dogs on either 2 short walks a day or 1 long walk

Don't walk them straight after they've had a meal if possible, there's a small chance this can make them ill

If Luna starts limping on her front left paw, don't worry it's a recurring corn and we will sort it when we get her home, she can go without the walks if she's limping a lot

Luna should stay on the lead on walks or in non-enclosed areas

Toilet routine

There are poo bags on the lead for when you're walking, -note: these are for the dogs-

We tend to let them out into our garden at least every 4 hours in the day, close the door and let them mooch about for 5 mins

If there is an accident, there is a cleaner in their bag, follow instructions on that
