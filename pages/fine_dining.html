Meal routine

These girls love to eat dinner, here's their normal routine

    Both:
  • Dentastick in the morning whenever you get up, (they know what is coming and will be excited) give it to them once they are outside and leave them out for a bit
  • They drink when they feel like it (Nymmy can usually find her bowl but might need a bit of help the first time in a new place), just make sure their bowls have water
  • Snacks whenever you like, they are very useful for bribing them to do things, for example: getting off furniture
    Nym:
  • Breakfast after dentastick, 1 of the tupperware containers of food
  • Dinner at 6pm, 1 of the tupperwear containers of food
    Luna:
  • Luna needs to eat her food and water (apart from dentastick) from higher up using the bowl stand
  • Breakfast after dentastick, 1 of the tupperware containers of food and 1 mugfull of biscuits (the mug is in the bag of biscuits)
  • Dinner at 6pm, 1 of the tupperware containers of food and 1 mugfull of biscuits
